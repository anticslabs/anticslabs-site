Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Antics Labs™ collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our app, PuttPuttPro™.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: Used to detect nearby mini-golf courses and improve course suggestions. This is only accessed while the app is in use and never stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We use analytics (Amplitude) to understand how users interact with the app and to improve features.
  • Advertising Data: We show ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID for personalized ads, in compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to provide and improve the app experience, suggest nearby courses, track gameplay stats, and support our ability to keep the app free via ads.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Amplitude (Analytics)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

Data Retention

We do not collect or store personal data such as names or email addresses. Usage data is retained by Amplitude and AdMob as per their policies.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access in your device settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device’s ad settings.
